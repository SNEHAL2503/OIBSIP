Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was one of India's most respected scientists and the 11th President of India. Known as the "Missile Man of India", he played a major role in India's space and defense programs.

I admire Dr. Kalam for his simplicity, dedication, and inspirational thoughts. He motivated millions of students to dream big and work hard to achieve success. His life teaches us that knowledge, discipline, and perseverance are the keys to greatness.

"Dream, Dream, Dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action." – Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam
